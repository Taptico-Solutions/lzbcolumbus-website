## Section 1: Post-Holiday Design Tips
**Title:** Refresh & Reset: Post-Holiday Design Tips
**Body:**
The decorations are packed away, and your home might feel a little... empty. But don't worry! This is the perfect time to reset your space for a fresh start.

*   **Embrace the "Blank Slate":** Use this time to deep clean and declutter. Let your room breathe before bringing in new decor.
*   **Layer on the Warmth:** Winter is still here! Add texture with chunky knit throws, velvet pillows, and plush rugs to keep things cozy.
*   **Light It Up:** With shorter days, lighting is key. Add a floor lamp to a dark corner or swap cool-toned bulbs for warm, inviting ones.

## Section 2: New Year's Resolutions for Your Home
**Title:** 3 Resolutions for a Better Home in 2025
**Body:**
This year, let's make resolutions that stick—and make your life more comfortable.

2.  **"I will define my style."** Not sure if you're "Modern Farmhouse" or "Classic Traditional"? Visit our showroom and let our designers help you find your unique look.
