<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Modern Farmhouse Chic
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Warmth & Welcome**: The site should feel like walking into a cozy, well-loved home.
  2. **Texture & Tactility**: Emphasize the feeling of fabrics and materials through visual design.
  3. **Family-Centric**: Highlight the Evans family story and community connection as the heart of the brand.
  4. **Curated Comfort**: Present furniture not just as products, but as essential elements of a comfortable life.

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  A palette inspired by natural materials and soft home interiors. 
  - **Primary**: Sage Green (#8DA399) - representing growth, nature, and calm.
  - **Secondary**: Warm Cream (#F5F5F0) - a soft, inviting background.
  - **Accent**: Terracotta/Clay (#C78D75) - adding warmth and a touch of earthiness.
  - **Text**: Charcoal Grey (#333333) - softer than black for readability.
  - **Metallic**: Brushed Brass (#D4AF37) - for elegant touches on buttons and borders.

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  Asymmetric, magazine-style layout. Avoid rigid grids. Use overlapping elements (images over text boxes) to create depth. Section dividers should be organic (curved lines or torn paper edges) rather than straight lines.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Fabric Swatch Textures**: Subtle background patterns resembling linen or woven fabric.
  2. **Handwritten Accents**: Use a script font for "Welcome," signatures, and key emphasis words to add a personal touch.
  3. **Polaroid-style Frames**: For family and community photos to enhance the nostalgic/personal feel.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  Gentle and fluid. Hover effects should be slow fades or slight lifts, mimicking the settling into a comfortable chair. Buttons should have a "soft" press effect.

  **Animation**:
  - **Scroll**: Parallax effects on background textures.
  - **Entrance**: Elements should "fade up" gently, like morning light.
  - **Hover**: Images zoom slightly and soften in focus.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Playfair Display* (Serif) - Elegant, classic, and editorial.
  - **Body**: *Lato* (Sans-serif) - Clean, friendly, and highly readable.
  - **Accents**: *Dancing Script* - For personal touches and signatures.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Soft Minimalist Luxury
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Elevated Simplicity**: Focus on high-quality imagery and negative space to suggest luxury.
  2. **Serene Atmosphere**: The site should evoke a sense of peace and relaxation.
  3. **Detail-Oriented**: Small details (lines, icons, spacing) are meticulously crafted.
  4. **Feminine Strength**: A design that is soft but confident, appealing to the modern female decision-maker.

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  A sophisticated, monochromatic palette with subtle shifts in tone.
  - **Primary**: Dusty Rose (#D8B4A0) - sophisticated, not girlish.
  - **Secondary**: Alabaster White (#FAFAFA) - clean and bright.
  - **Accent**: Muted Gold (#C5A059) - for luxury cues.
  - **Text**: Slate Blue-Grey (#4A5568) - professional and modern.
  - **Background**: Very light warm grey (#F7F7F7).

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  Split-screen layouts and large hero imagery. Use plenty of whitespace to let the furniture "breathe." Content sections should flow vertically with generous padding.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Thin Line Art**: Delicate illustrations of furniture outlines or botanical elements.
  2. **Circular Masks**: Use circular or arch-shaped image masks to break up the rectangular nature of screens.
  3. **Floating Cards**: Content boxes with deep, soft shadows to create a layered effect.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  Refined and precise. Interactions should feel "expensive" - snappy but smooth.

  **Animation**:
  - **Scroll**: Elements slide in from the sides.
  - **Entrance**: Staggered reveal of text and images.
  - **Hover**: Buttons fill with color from left to right; images desaturate or shift to sepia.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Cormorant Garamond* (Serif) - High contrast, stylish, and feminine.
  - **Body**: *Montserrat* (Sans-serif) - Geometric but approachable.
  - **Accents**: *Allura* - For elegant callouts.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Organic Contemporary
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Nature-Inspired**: Bringing the outdoors in, reflecting the trend of biophilic design.
  2. **Fluidity**: Shapes and layouts that flow naturally, avoiding sharp corners.
  3. **Authenticity**: Focus on real materials, real people, and real comfort.
  4. **Vibrant Comfort**: Using color to express the joy of a comfortable home.

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  Earthy but vibrant.
  - **Primary**: Deep Teal (#2A6F6F) - rich and grounding.
  - **Secondary**: Sand (#E6DDC4) - warm neutral.
  - **Accent**: Coral (#FF7F50) - energetic and friendly.
  - **Text**: Dark Brown (#3E2723) - warm alternative to black.
  - **Background**: Off-white with subtle grain.

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  Broken grid layout. Images and text overlap boundaries. Use of organic shapes (blobs, waves) as background dividers.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Organic Shapes**: Background blobs in soft colors to frame content.
  2. **Botanical Motifs**: Leaf and vine illustrations peeking from corners.
  3. **Textured Typography**: Headlines with a subtle texture overlay.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  Playful and bouncy. Interactions should feel alive.

  **Animation**:
  - **Scroll**: Elements float upwards with a slight bounce.
  - **Entrance**: Scale-up animations for images.
  - **Hover**: Icons wiggle or rotate slightly.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Merriweather* (Serif) - Readable and sturdy but with character.
  - **Body**: *Open Sans* (Sans-serif) - Neutral and friendly.
  - **Accents**: *Pacifico* - Fun and handwritten for special offers.
</idea>
