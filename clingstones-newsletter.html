The Stones Are Coming Home.
Opening Night is April 3rd.

The wait is almost over. The Columbus Clingstones — Columbus's own Double-A affiliate of the Atlanta Braves — are back at Synovus Park, and we couldn't be more excited.

As a proud sponsor of the Clingstones, La-Z-Boy of Columbus is thrilled to cheer on our hometown team all season long. Whether you're a die-hard baseball fan or just looking for a great night out with the family, there's nothing quite like a summer evening at the ballpark.

Our Partnership

La-Z-Boy × Clingstones

Your Kid's Own
Field of Dreams.

"We want to give every young player in the Chattahoochee Valley the chance to walk out onto that field and feel like a hero."

Through our partnership with the Clingstones, La-Z-Boy of Columbus is sponsoring the Field of Dreams program — giving local youth league teams the incredible experience of taking the field at Synovus Park alongside their heroes.

Youth teams get to run out of the dugout, stand on the same diamond as the pros, and create memories that last a lifetime. It's one of our favorite things we've ever done as part of this community.

We're also proud to sponsor the La-Z-Boy of Columbus Player of the Week — recognizing standout performances throughout the season.
